12. Explain the significance of satellite DNA in DNA fingerprinting technique.
Solution:
Satelite DNA are the repetitive DNA sequences which show high degree of polymorphism and form the
basis of DNA fingerprinting since DNA from every tissue (such as blood, hair folicle, skin bone, saliva etc)
from an individual show the same degree of polymorphism, they become very useful identification tool in
forensic applications. As the polymorphisms are inheritable from parents to children DNA finger printing is
the basis of paterenity testing, in case of disputes.
