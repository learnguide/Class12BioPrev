Parthenogenesis : In this process haploid egg cell of female gametophyte is responsible to form a haploid
embryo without fertilization.
Apogamy : In this process any haploid cell of female gametophyte except egg cell is responsible to form a
haploid embryo without fertilization.
Parthenogenesis and Apogamy both are not included in agamospermy.
