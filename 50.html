Describe the mutual relationship between fig tree and wasp and comment on the phenomenon that
operates in their relationship.
Solution:
Mutualism is present between wasp and fig tree. female wasp lays its egg inside developing fruit of fig tree.
Female wasp use fig fruit as oviposition site and also use its developing seed for nourishing its larva. The
wasp pollinate the fig inflorescence while searching for suitable site for egg laying.
Thus fig and wasp both get benefited from this phenomenon.
